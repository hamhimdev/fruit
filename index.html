    Fruit

    Fruit is still being updated. We are aware that all the categories are not filled.

    Welcome to fruit.

    • A large and extensive hub for all designer and developer needs. Everything here is 100% free.
    • You can help us by sending Pull Requests to add more services or by removing ones whose offerings have changed or been retired.
    • Please do note if you find an issue with any service listed here, report it to them, not us! (we didn't make it, we are only listing it!)
    • This project is inspired by free-for.dev! Check them out!

    What to do in Pull Requests.

    • Please refer to this page when adding icons.
    • If it is not available there, keep a similar icon with it, for example if Gmail's icon is not there, you can use:
    • Google (Material) :material-google:
    • Google (Fontawesome) :fontawesome-brands-google:
    • Google (Simple) :simple-google:
    • A short description about the service, preferrably the first thing you see when you load up said service's site should be there.
    • The service should also have a free tier that does not compromise security.
    • The service can be a personal project of yours, as long as it has reasonable uptime.

    Communication services

    • Discord - A place that makes it easy to talk every day and hang out more often.
    • Guilded - Guilded upgrades your group chat and equips your server with integrated event calendars, forums, and more – 100% free.
    • Skype - A popular platform in it's old days. Now owned by Microsoft.
    • TeamSpeak - Use crystal clear sound to communicate with your team mates cross-platform with military-grade security, lag-free performance & unparalleled reliability and uptime.

    Email services

    • Gmail - Gmail is email that's intuitive, efficient, and useful.
    • Outlook - Outlook is a free, personal email service.
    • Proton Mail - Keep your conversations private. Proton Mail is an encrypted email service based in Switzerland.
    • yahoo!mail - Get stuff done, with a whopping 1TB of free storage, and utterlessly useful features!
